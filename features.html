Flaxseed

Often misunderstood as just a fiber source, flaxseed is one of the richest plant-based sources of alpha-linolenic acid (ALA), an essential omega-3 fatty acid. It supports cardiovascular health and helps maintain a healthy inflammatory response, making it a cornerstone of any natural health supplement regimen.

Chia Seed

A staple in sports nutrition supplements, chia seeds are hydrophilic marvels that absorb up to 12 times their weight in water. This gel-forming action aids in prolonged hydration and slow-release energy, while their complete protein profile and high calcium content support muscle recovery and bone density without dairy.

Sesame Seed

Beyond the decorative sprinkle on buns, sesame seeds are a dense source of copper, manganese, and calcium.
